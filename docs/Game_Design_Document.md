# Operation: Overkill - Game Design Document

## Vision

A fast, explosive 2D side-scrolling action shooter inspired by the
pacing and destructibility of Broforce, but with original characters,
world, mechanics and identity.

## Pillars

-   Responsive controls first.
-   Spectacular destruction.
-   Every operative feels unique.
-   Short, replayable missions.
-   Constant rewards and progression.
-   Polish over feature bloat.

## Core Loop

Main Menu -\> World Map -\> Mission Briefing -\> Mission -\> Extraction
-\> Rewards -\> Unlocks -\> Next Mission

## Main Menu

Animated battlefield backdrop, helicopter flyovers, smoke, lightning,
radio chatter. Buttons: - Campaign - Continue - Armory - Operatives -
Challenges - Settings - Credits - Exit

## Mission Select

Interactive world map with regions. Mission cards show: - Name -
Difficulty - Objectives - Secrets - Best Time - Rating

## Gameplay

Movement: - Run - Jump - Unlockable Double Jump - Roll - Slide -
Grappling Hook - Wall Jump - Rocket Jump

Combat: Weapons: - Pistol - SMG - Shotgun - Sniper - Rocket Launcher -
Grenade Launcher - Flamethrower - Minigun - Laser Rifle - Railgun

Gadgets: Grenades, EMP, Flashbang, Smoke, Molotov, Mine, Turret, Drone,
Health Injector.

## Operatives

Each has unique stats, primary weapon, gadget and passive. Examples: -
Phantom - Titan - Volt - Blaze - Ranger - Boomer

## Enemies

Rifleman, Shotgunner, Sniper, Shield, Flamethrower, Jetpack, Heavy,
Tank, Helicopter, Mech.

## Boss Design

Mechanic-driven bosses with weak points and cinematic finishers.

## Controls

A/D Move Space Jump Shift Roll Ctrl Slide Left Mouse Fire Right Mouse
Aim/Alt Fire R Reload E Interact G Grenade Q Gadget 1 F Gadget 2 T
Grappling Hook 1-5 Weapon Slots Mouse Wheel Weapon Cycle Tab Objectives
Esc Pause

## Progression

Credits, stars, unlockable operatives, weapon upgrades, mastery,
achievements, New Game+.

## Secrets

Hidden rooms, breakable walls, collectibles, easter eggs, secret bosses.

## Polish

Screen shake, hit stop, dynamic music, shell casings, sparks, scorch
marks, weather, searchlights, camera zooms, adaptive soundtrack.

## Accessibility

Remappable controls, subtitles, aim assist, colorblind options, toggles
for blood and screen shake.

## Additional Features

-   Dynamic difficulty that subtly adjusts enemy aggression.
-   Elite enemy variants.
-   Combo meter rewarding stylish play.
-   Daily challenge modifiers.
-   Mission medals.
-   Replay system for speedruns.
-   Photo mode.
-   Unlockable lore, soundtrack and concept art.
-   Random ambient events (airstrikes, collapsing bridges, storms).
-   Environmental hazards (electric water, explosive pipes, lava,
    icicles).
-   Companion drone unlock.
-   End-of-level extraction sequence every mission.
